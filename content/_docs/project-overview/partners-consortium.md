The UniMaaS consortium brings together **18 partners from 9 European countries plus Canada**, combining multidisciplinary competencies from academia, industry, and research communities:

**Coordinator:**
- **NTUA** (National Technical University of Athens, Greece) - Project Coordinator

**Academic & Research Partners:**
- **QUB** (The Queen's University of Belfast, UK)
- **UCLouvain** (Université Catholique de Louvain, Belgium)
- **INRIA** (Institut National de Recherche en Informatique et Automatique, France)
- **TUB** (Technische Universität Berlin, Germany)
- **UPV** (Universitat Politècnica de València, Spain)
- **ETS** (École de Technologie Supérieure, Canada)
- **FLM** (Flanders Make, Belgium)
- **LUK** (Sieć Badawcza Łukasiewicz, Poland)

**Technology Providers:**
- **NET** (Netcompany-Intrasoft SA, Luxembourg)
- **NET-BE** (Netcompany-Intrasoft, Belgium)
- **CEL** (Research & Innovation Partner)
- **ODINS** (Technology SME)
- **FDI** (Technology SME)

**Industrial Pilots:**
- **AEGEAN** (Aegean Airlines, Greece) - Aircraft Maintenance
- **ADIENT** (Adient Ltd & Co KG, Germany) - Automotive Seating
- **ANV** (Additive Manufacturing SME) - 3D Construction Printing
- **CATONE** (Logistics Company) - Warehouse Management

This diverse consortium ensures comprehensive expertise across data management, modeling, decision-making, and vertical industry applications.[1]
