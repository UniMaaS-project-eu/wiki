## Core Suites

The UniMaaS platform is organized into three core suites covering data management, modelling, and decision‑making capabilities:

- Data Management (WP4): Data Spaces & Knowledge Graph, Cloud‑based Resource Monitoring, Trusted Data Exchange, Digital Product Passport
- Modelling (WP5): Formal Models, AI‑based Predictors, Intent‑based Servitization, Zero‑X Evaluation
- Decision‑Making (WP6): Resource Scheduling & Planning, Digital Twins, Cloud Manufacturing Orchestration, Circularity & Sustainability Optimization
